Understanding Musical Terms

This glossary defines key musical concepts referenced throughout the website. Understanding these terms will help you better appreciate how different musical elements combine to create specific emotional and cognitive responses.

🎹

Major Key

Musical Theory
A major key is a musical key based on a major scale, which generally sounds bright, stable, and positive to listeners.
⏰

Tempo

Timing
The speed of a song, usually measured in beats per minute (BPM).
📊

Dynamics

Volume
The loudness or softness of music and how it changes over time.
🎸

Instrumentation

Sound Source
The instruments used in a piece of music.
🎵

Harmony

Musical Structure
How multiple notes or chords are played together.
🎜

Mode (Major / Minor)

Scale Type
The scale type used in a song.
🥁

Rhythm

Beat Pattern
The pattern of beats in music.
🌊

Texture

Density
How thick or thin the sound is (few instruments vs many).
🎨

Timbre

Tone Color
The tone colour of a sound — why a piano and guitar sound different even on the same note.
